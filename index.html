Missions to Mars

Mars Pathfinder

Launched in 1996, Mars Pathfinder was NASA's first rover mission to Mars, exploring the Martian terrain and transmitting valuable data back to Earth.

Opportunity Rover

Part of NASA's Mars Exploration Rover mission, Opportunity landed on Mars in 2004 and operated well beyond its expected service life, making significant discoveries about water on Mars.

Curiosity Rover

Launched by NASA in 2011, the Curiosity Rover was tasked with investigating Mars' climate and geology and assessing whether the planet could have supported microbial life.

Perseverance Rover

NASA's Perseverance Rover, part of the Mars 2020 mission, landed on Mars in February 2021. Its mission includes searching for signs of ancient life and collecting samples of rock and regolith (broken rock and soil) for possible return to Earth.
